descriptionZh: |
  在作品《白色大地》中，我實地考察了曾是台灣重要開採白土壙之地：北投的貴子坑並撿拾了白色的石頭，後續透過一系列的加工，重新的燒製成各種陶瓷。這些陶瓷小雕塑造型包括石頭、花、樹葉造型。作品還包括一本書，書裡的文字提及我對搜集材料的經歷以及歷史資料閱讀到的啟發。以書籍的方式放在展覽現場提供人翻閱。
descriptionEn: |
  In the work <em>White Ground</em>, I visited 貴子坑 in Beitou, which was once an important white clay mining site in Taiwan. There I gathered white stones that were then re-fired into various ceramics through a series of processes. These ceramic figurines include stone, flower, and leaf shapes. The installation also includes a book, containing text that narrates my material-collecting journey and draws inspiration from historical sources. This book was placed at the exhibition for visitors to browse.
