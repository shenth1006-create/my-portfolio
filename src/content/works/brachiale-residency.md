descriptionZh: |
  「Brachiale」是奧地利首個慶祝荒地的藝術節（德語為 Brache），舉辦地點為 Zukunftshof，這是一座歷史悠久但現已荒廢的舊莊園。策展團隊由視覺藝術家 Lisa Bolyos 和 Lisbeth Kovacic 組成。藝術節「Brachiale」的目標是追蹤並理解荒地的各種形態，包括荒地的植物、聲音，甚至對無用之處的觀察。為此，他們邀請了來自表演藝術和視覺藝術領域的10位藝術家及藝術團體進行駐村創作，各自提出不同的提案，並於2024年5月25日與26日舉辦公眾活動及成果發表。

  Zukunftshof 位於維也納市郊，是一座古老的莊園。該莊園的農場於2015年關閉，並出售給維也納房屋基金會，目前暫時由 Zukunftshof 協會用於發展永續食品項目和文化宣傳用途。「Brachiale」藝術節的駐村創作與藝術節活動將在此舉辦，重新利用這個富有歷史意義的空間，通過藝術與創意賦予其新生命。
descriptionEn: |
  "Brachiale" is Austria's first art festival celebrating wastelands (referred to in German as Brache). It is held at Zukunftshof, a historic yet now-abandoned old estate. The curatorial team consists of visual artists Lisa Bolyos and Lisbeth Kovacic. The aim of the "Brachiale" festival is to trace and understand the various forms of wastelands, including their plants, sounds, and even observations on the notion of uselessness. For this purpose, 10 artists and art collectives from the fields of performance and visual arts were invited for residency, each proposing their unique take. Public activities and presentations were held on May 25th and 26th, 2024.

  Zukunftshof is an old manor located on the outskirts of Vienna. The estate's farm was closed in 2015 and sold to the Vienna Housing Foundation. It is currently being temporarily used by the Zukunftshof Association for sustainable food initiatives and cultural promotion. The artist residency and "Brachiale" festival took place at this historically significant space, revitalizing it through art and creativity.
